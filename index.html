What We Do

Our Landscaping Services

From precision paving to complete backyard transformations — every project gets the same obsessive attention to detail.

Paving & Relaying

From new paved entertaining areas to relaying large existing sections, we handle every type of paving project with precision. Whether it's a driveway, courtyard, or garden path, we ensure proper drainage, solid base preparation, and a flawless finish.

Pool Surrounds & Travertine Repairs

We specialise in pool area transformations — including cracked travertine repairs, resealing, and complete pool surround installations. Your pool area cops a beating from the elements, so we use the right techniques and materials to ensure it lasts.

Retaining Walls & Structural Work

Geelong's varied terrain often demands smart retaining wall solutions to create usable outdoor space. We design and build retaining walls that are structurally sound, compliant with Australian Standards, and look like they belong on your property.

Complete Landscape Transformations

We take tired, underused backyards and turn them into outdoor spaces you'll actually want to spend time in. From concept through to planting, lighting, and finishing touches, we manage the whole project so you deal with one person — Jaryd.

Outdoor Entertainment Areas

Geelong weather is made for entertaining outdoors, and we build the spaces to match — think alfresco areas, fire pits, and decking. Every project is tailored to how your family actually lives, not pulled from a template.
